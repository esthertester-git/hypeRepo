Contrast Make important ideas stand out. Use size, colour, or bold text to show what matters most on each slide.

Repetition Use consistent fonts, colours, and layouts throughout. Consistency signals professionalism and makes your deck easier to follow.

Alignment Line up your text and visual components deliberately. Random placement looks unprofessional and makes slides harder to read.

Proximity Group related components together. White space between unrelated components helps the audience understand what belongs together.
